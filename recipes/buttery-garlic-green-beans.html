Buttery Garlic Green Beans

Description

Green bean recipes make the greatest side dishes. They take very little time to cook and, depending on the method, even less time to prep. This easy green bean recipe preps in just 5 minutes, cooks in about 10 (depending on how crunchy you like your green beans), and is ready in 15 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound fresh green beans, trimmed
  • 5 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

Steps and Instructions

  1. Simmer: Bring the green beans to a boil, then reduce to a simmer until they're just tender. For this green bean recipe, it's better to undercook slightly than overcook. Drain the green beans and return them to the skillet. The residual heat will continue to cook the green beans.
  2. Saut√©: Add butter to the hot skillet. Add minced garlic and cook for 1 to 2 minutes, making sure the green beans are coated with the garlicy butter. Season and serve.
